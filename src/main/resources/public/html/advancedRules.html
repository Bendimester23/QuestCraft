Rules

Basic Rules:

Administration of the server is not responsible for any losses during any server issues, caused by players connection, nor main server problems We'll try to do everything possible to avoid that or inform players about the problems if it is possible.

QuestCraft Server is a non-commercial server with the possibility of freewill donations All costs are spent on the upgrade of the server No donations return possible.

Administration of the server has a right to interrupt in any processes on the server Decisions on the server are made up through the public voting, however, the administration has the last word for it.

The administration has no right to restore any lost, due to the player's fault, items However, it is encouraged to report any cases of griefing, fraud, cheating, hack or exchange without a representative of the administration. (We believe our players are trustful people, through there might be exceptions.)

All players are required to have the Discord and be in the QuestCraft Server Discord Channel, as without it, the player would not experience even 20% of the SMP life.

Chat Rules:

It is forbidden to make up conflicts based on religion, nationality. Punishment - mute for 1 hour.

It is forbidden to write messages that do not have any sense in them Punishment - mute for 30 minutes.

It is forbidden to send identical or similar messages, as much as usage of the five identical symbols repeated in a row
Punishment - mute from 15 minutes to 6 hours.

It is forbidden to send aggressive messages, provocations, mocking and threats of any kind
Punishment - mute from 1 to 6 hours.

It is forbidden to use Caps (Up Register) in the messages. Exceptions - Names, first letters, abbreviations, text smiles. Punishment - mute for 30 minutes.

It is forbidden to introduce oneself as part of the administration group or make any false statements about the QuestCraft Server.
Punishment - mute for 1 hour.

It is forbidden to violate the rules that have warnings as the first measure in response for violation (Up to 3 violations repeatedly, does not include punishments for violations themselves.)
Punishment - mute from 1 to 6 hours.

Usage of the profanity (bad language) is prohibited Profanity includes any bad words in any language (Administration knows at least 6 the most popular), veiled filthy language, rude, abusive, and abusive language that goes beyond cultural communication.
Punishment - mute from 30 minutes to 6 hours.

It is forbidden to insult players and humiliate their human dignity. Punishment - mute from 1 hour to 1 day (24 hours) Punishment may be increased, depending on the severity of the violation.

It is forbidden to write messages of an erotic nature.
Punishment - mute for 1 hour.
Punishment - mute for 15 minutes.

Any insult or mention of the relatives of the QuestCraft Server players in an insulting form is prohibited. Punishment - mute from 7 to 14 days.

It is forbidden to discuss and criticize any actions of the administration, rules and the QuestCraft Server as a whole.
Punishment - temporary or permanent ban (depends on the decision of the administration).

It is forbidden to misinform and mislead players about the gameplay.
Punishment - mute for 30 minutes.

Advertising of any Minecraft projects or servers is prohibited. Exceptions - mentions of those are not counted as advertising, therefore not prohibited. Punishment - permanent ban.

It is forbidden to mention narcotic drugs, Nazi ideas, suicide, the use of criminal (prison) jargon Note - may be some exceptions (Depends on the judgement of administration). Punishment - mute for 1 hour

It is forbidden to leave the server immediately after violating the rules of the project (to avoid punishment)
Punishment - if the player does not log on to the server within five minutes - tempban for the tripled time specified in the paragraph of the rules that have been violated. Note - if a player is not able to log on to the server in 5 minutes for a special reason (server issues), it should be reported in QuestCraft Server Discord.

Prefixes that violate any chat rules are prohibited. Punishment - mute for 30 minutes, delete of the prefix.

Gaming Process:

It is forbidden to destabilize the server, cause falls, create lags / lagzones
Punishment - demontage of the mechanism (resources will not be returned), ban for 7 days.

It is forbidden to trap players for killing.
Punishment - jail from 3 hours up to tempban for 7 days.

Any kind of cheating/fraud is prohibited
Punishment - if the offender does not correct what he has done - tempban for 7 days up to permban.

It is forbidden to harm the buildings of other players by pouring liquids, dropping quick blocks, building walls, fences and digging ditches near private houses, creating back-to-back buildings that are a hindrance as well as the destruction of the building itself (in exception of administration, that destroyed building with a reason (administrator who did it will be asked a reason and punished accordingly in the case of a violation).
Punishment - jail from 6 hours up to tempban for 7 days.

It is forbidden to arrange any kind of gambling in any of their manifestations (casinos, roulettes, etc.) if it is not a business Building a casino would be encouraged in all means
Punishment - jail up to 4 hours.

It is forbidden to distribute and use game holes/malfunctions/bugs/dupes/malware to harm the server or get advantages in the game with their help Any software that interferes with the game/client in any way is also prohibited (Maybe exception a.k.a TNT duper etc.)
Punishment - from 7 days tempban up to permban.

It is forbidden to install skins/capes containing: erotic elements, Nazi symbols, insults, mats, prison slang
Punishment - the player is given 1 hour to change the skin/cape At the expiration of the term - the prison until the moment when the player does not change the skin/cape.

It is forbidden to interfere with a player while in his region (building) If the owner of the territory or the moderator asked you to leave the territory, you must obey this Note - rule does not apply on an administration that does the administration tasks.
Punishment - jail for 30 minutes.

It is forbidden to violate any rules of events The rules are indicated on the event place signs.
Punishment - jail for 1 hour.

It is forbidden to interfere with the moderator, who holds events, exchanges, monitors the PvP
Punishment - jail for 30 minutes

In the names of mobs or animals, in registered scrolls, as well as in the names of blocks and objects are forbidden: mats, insults, advertising of other projects, terms of a sexual nature.
Punishment - jail for 6 hours, removal of animal/ mob/ item.

It is forbidden to deliberately misinform/mislead the administration/moderation of the project for any benefit or other selfish purposes.
Punishment - tempban for 1 - 7 days.

Pranks are allowed on the QuestCraft, however, they must not violate the QuestCraft Server Rules and pranked person must be ok with the prank. Punishment in case of bad intuition (the wrong person pranked) - punishment correspondingly with The Rules (if any violated),
punishment correspondingly with the damage caused by the prank (Based on the administration.)

Building:

It is forbidden to build genitals, swear words and insults, fascist symbols, and also to install signs in your region containing filthy language/ insults.
Punishment - from jail for 5 hours up to tempban for 3 days, demontage of the building.

It is forbidden to build houses without supports, hanging in the sky and not obeying the laws of physics unless the building has a specific topic (ex.: UFO).
Punishment - correction time for 24 hours If the player does not correct during this time - 5 hours in prison and demolition of the building.

It is forbidden to build pillars into the sky, houses of "boxes" consisting entirely of one material If your home is under construction, set a sign to let us know! Regions whose owner has been absent from the game for more than 30 days are deleted and regenerated.
Punishment - demolition of the building.

Discord Server Rules:

It is forbidden to use the mat, veiled mat, mat in other languages, the use of other profanity Obscene words and phrases written with the intentional replacement, deletion or separation of characters are equivalent to profanity.
Punishment - deletion of the post, excluding from every channel for 12 hours.

It is forbidden to insult the project players/administration. Punishment - deletion of the post, tempban on the Minecraft Server for 1 day. Advertising of third-party resources not related to the QuestCraft Server is prohibited.
Punishment - permban.

Economy:

Help within the player community is accepted, however, help must be acquired in Survival Gamemode. (Administration.)
Punishment - tempban for 14 days (3 in case of the solving the way illegal resources were acquired)(for players), questioning the trustfulness of the administration up to removal from administration group (for admins).

It is prohibited to set the price for the items in the shops lower than stated in the economics of the server.
Punishment - blocking of the shop until price fix.

Administrator part:

Each QuestCraft Server admin is obliged to consider complaints on the Discord Server, monitor the order in the chat and control the gameplay.

The admin has no right to ignore offences of any players and other moderators.

Before punishing a player, the admin must carefully understand the situation.

The admin does not have the right to use the rights granted to him for personal purposes, to violate the rules of the project or to interpret them in his favour or benefit to a third party.

The admin is obliged to answer a question posed to him that directly relates to the game process, even if the question violates the chat rules.

The admin is not required to teach anyone the game.

The admin is obliged to issue warnings before punishment.

The admin is obliged to take screenshots of players' violations and keep them for a week.

The admin is obliged to behave decently and respectfully with the players and other moderators in the realities of the project, as well as comply with the framework of the communication culture.

Any member of staff does not have the right to distribute information that he received from moderation conferences in Discord All information that should be received by the players will be announced in the corresponding channel in
