About

QuestCraft


QuestCraft is a small survival minecraft server that was first released on Tuesday, July 9th, 2019. originally created by chestly with insparation from Simple Survival and HermitCraft. As it grows it continues to gain more popularity not only among the administrators friends, but also on planet minecraft.

As a early server QuestCraft was mainly based of the idea of a place where players could come, and not face internet drama, and have a respectful place to build make friends and have fun.

Recently with 1.15 released and now 1.15.1-1.15.2 which we have upgraded too, we have released a new world and we are continuing to grow at a even faster rate that before. with teh new world comes a whitelist to keep out players who shouldnt be part of our community.
